- **Bending moment strategy** : for the arms, the max. bending moment caused by the thrust (applied load) will be at the root (centerplate), while its minimum is at the motors. Thus, we can taper the arms to make them thinner at the propellers !

- **Unibody vs. Modular** : we'll go for modular aka multiple pieces (4 arms, 1 center plate) screwed together for easier repair/replacement. This will cost slightly more in 3d printing ¨

- **EDIT** : the centerplate is not ideal, we'll change it to a rectangular centerplate, to have more space for all holes + components. The battery below will rule the weight repartition by sliding along that rectangle til dead center CG
